Java 1.15.2 is coming this week

1.15.2 pre-1

Snapshot for: 1.15.2

1.15.2 will have many small changes, along with some notable ones.

  • Farmable honeycomb! A hopper underneath a nest/hive will now collect honeycomb when it is sheared using a dispenser.
    • Technical: Items are now dropped out as a cluster (one entity) instead of a separate entity for each item of honeycomb.
  • If a beacon gives you an effect of a higher level than an otherwise-identical effect that you already have, the old effect will now tick away in the background and resume once you go out of range of the beacon.
    • Technical: Status effects now have a tag which holds the data of the same effect at a lower level.
  • Bee nests are now renewable! If a tree grows from a sapling that has any flower placed within two blocks of it (on the same y-level) it has a 2% chance to spawn with a bee nest in it.
  • Any tree naturally generated in a biome Flower Forest now has a 2% chance of growing with a bee nest.
  • Trees now have a 0.2% chance to generate with bee nests in the following biomes: Forest, Wooded Hills, Birch Forest, Tall Birch Forest, Birch Forest Hills, and Tall Birch Hills.
  • Two new gamerules: DoPatrolSpawning and DoTraderSpawning: when false, (default true) they disable the spawning of Pillager raids / Wondering Traders.
  • Two resource pack changes. In the words of Minecraft.net: “Added gui_light option in block models to allow controlling light when rendering model as item in GUI. Controls light when rendering block model inside slot. If set to side, model will be rendered like block. If set to front, model is shaded like flat item.”.
  • Spawn protection on servers now applies to item frames, paintings and armour stands.
  • You can no longer hold space to ‘fall’ down a ladder instead of climbing it.
  • Multiplayer now displays a warning screen.
    • Technical: The ‘Do not show this screen again’ option is stored as skipMultiplayerWarning on line 60 of options.txt.
  • Minecraft’s window title will now show whether you are playing Singleplayer, Multiplayer or Multiplayer (LAN); and whether it is modded or not.
  • Profiler reports are now more detailed.

Notable bug fixes

  • 2 bugs regarding converting concrete powder.
  • Bees will now pop out when you break a bee hive with an obstructed front.
  • Bees that go into hives/nests after being in a boat/minecart can now come out again.
  • Bees can now only pollinate up to ten crops on their way to their hive/nest.
  • Furnace minecarts no longer lose power after turning corners. (Srsly who actually uses furnace minecarts?)
  • Bed proxemics have be[e/a]n fixed.
  • Crit. Hit particles and horse armour where off-centred or something.
  • The cracked overlay on iron golems used to be visible when the golem was invisible.
  • End crystal beams’ particles and looms’ pattern previews have been brightened.
  • Fixed capitalisation of some menu buttons.
  • Invisible and glowing entities now respect their team colour.
  • Crash and exploit fixes.

Compatibility/bugs

  • Optifine – No.
  • Forge – No.
  • Backwards-compatibility: SP: 1.15 & 1.15.1
  • Resource packs will break.
  • As per usual, tell me if you encounter a problem.
